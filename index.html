Spring has turned into Summer! The sun is warm, the beaches beckon, and seagulls let go of their troubles on unsuspecting cats. Watch your heads!

The Silver-Light welcomes all to a season long beach party- get ready for fun in the sun! He asks, specifically, no one bring conch shells up in his presence. It's starting to get hot- really hot- stay cool out there!

Pick a spot at the nearest beach, collect some shells (not infront of Sitka), and enjoy the good weather!

For now.

Theme: Summer Fun

Color Palette: Blue, Green, Brown (Beach Day)

Style/Medium: Painted/Abstract

We are an RPG group that operates with Dungeons, quests, EXP and skill upgrading among others. We hope to provide a safe and fun environment for all our members, as well as a good story-telling experience.

Feel free to join our Discord and browse through some of our lore and journals! We hope to one day see you wandering the paths of Corondryx alongside us.
